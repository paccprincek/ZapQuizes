zap-quizes(FIRE-EMOJI FIRE-EMOJI) Zap Quizzes—Play. Learn. Compete.

Welcome to Zap Quizzes where learning meets fun and competition meets curiosity! Think of it as your ultimate quiz arena — built for people who love testing knowledge with fun,challenging friends,and feeling that “aha!” moment when they get things right.

What’s Zap Quizzes? Zap Quizzes isn’t just another boring question app. It’s an interactive quiz platform that keeps you on your toes with:

A personal dashboard that shows your progress—what you’ve completed and what’s pending. A countdown timer-that keeps the thrill alive. Notifications and alerts-so you never miss your next quiz challenge. Multiple quiz topics— choose your area, from tech and movies to general knowledge. A create-your-own-quiz feature-where you can design questions, share them, and invite your friends to compete. Support for multi-player quizzes, so it’s not just “you vs. the quiz”… it’s you vs. everyone! 😎

Tech Stack Layer Technology Frontend-React.js Backend -Node.js with Express.js Database- MySQL Notifications Custom logic with timers & alerts Hosting- Vercel

How It Works Login or sign up — your journey starts with a profile that tracks your growth. Pick a quiz topic — from tech trivia to random fun facts. Start the quiz — you’ll see a live timer counting down as you answer. Finish strong! — get your score instantly and see where you rank. Create your own quizzes — add questions, set correct answers, and share your quiz link with friends. Challenge others — turn studying or free time into a fun brain battle.
